ABOUT YOUR AUTOMOTIVE CONSULTANT

Your Personal Car Advisor,
Not a Dealer.

Every automobile dealership in the world represents the seller. As your luxury car consultant, we represent you.

Provenance was founded on a simple premise: acquiring a luxury or exotic car should feel like working with a trusted automotive advisor, not negotiating across a showroom floor.

As Texas's dedicated car buying service, Provenance is run by people who deeply understand cars. Rather than just facilitating transactions, we serve collectors and enthusiasts across Houston, Dallas, Austin, San Antonio, Fort Worth, and all of Texas by leveraging our deep knowledge of classics, exotic supercars, modern bespoke options, and tough-to-find sourcing to offer an unmatched level of expertise and discretion.
