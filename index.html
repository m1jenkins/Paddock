ABOUT PROVENANCE

Your Advocate,
Not a Dealer.

Every automobile dealership in the world represents the seller. We represent you. Provenance was founded on a simple premise: acquiring a significant vehicle should feel like working with a trusted advisor, not negotiating across a showroom floor.

Crucially, Provenance is run by people who deeply understand cars. Rather than just facilitating transactions, we serve a national clientele of collectors and enthusiasts by leveraging our deep knowledge of classics, modern bespoke options, and tough-to-find sourcing to offer an unmatched level of expertise and discretion.
