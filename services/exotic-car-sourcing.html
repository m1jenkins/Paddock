Exotic & Supercar Sourcing

When the exact car you want doesn't appear on any listing, Provenance activates a global network to find and acquire it on your behalf.

Schedule a Free Consultation

Finding the Unfindable

The most desirable exotic cars rarely appear on public listings. A low-mile Ferrari 458 Speciale in the right specification, a Paint-to-Sample Porsche 911 GT3, a particular-spec McLaren 765LT — these vehicles trade hands through private networks, word-of-mouth, and relationships built over decades. Provenance exists at the center of these networks, giving our Texas clients access to vehicles they simply couldn't find on their own.

Our Global Sourcing Network

Provenance maintains relationships with collectors, dealers, brokers, and enthusiast communities across the United States and internationally. When a client needs a specific vehicle, we activate this network — reaching out to private owners who may not be actively selling, checking inventory at specialist dealers worldwide, monitoring auction consignment lists, and tapping into enthusiast forums and clubs where rare vehicles change hands quietly.

Marques & Models We Source

Our exotic car sourcing expertise spans the full range of high-performance and luxury vehicles. We regularly source Ferrari (488 Pista, F8 Tributo, SF90, 296 GTB, Roma, Purosangue), Porsche (911 GT3, GT3 RS, GT2 RS, 918 Spyder, Cayman GT4 RS), McLaren (750S, 720S, 765LT, Artura, Senna), Lamborghini (Huracán Tecnica, Huracán STO, Revuelto, Urus Performante), Aston Martin (Vantage, DB12, DBS), and Mercedes-AMG (GT Black Series, ONE, GT 63 S E Performance).

Thorough Vetting & Due Diligence

Finding the car is only half the battle. Before presenting any vehicle to a client, Provenance conducts thorough due diligence: comprehensive vehicle history reports, title verification, service record review, pre-purchase inspection by marque-specialist mechanics, paint depth measurement, and verification that the car's provenance matches the seller's claims. We protect you from costly mistakes that even experienced buyers can miss.

Expert Negotiation

Exotic car pricing is highly nuanced. Market values fluctuate based on specification, mileage, color combination, options, service history, and even the seller's motivation. Provenance's deep market knowledge ensures you pay the right price — not a dollar more. We've saved Texas clients significant sums by knowing exactly what a car is worth and negotiating accordingly.

Texas-Wide Service

Our exotic car sourcing service is available to buyers across Texas. Whether you're in Houston, Dallas, Austin, San Antonio, Fort Worth, Midland, or anywhere in between, Provenance brings the world's finest exotic cars to your doorstep.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Exotic & Supercar Sourcing — FAQ

How long does it take to source a specific exotic car?

Timelines vary depending on the rarity of the vehicle. Common models like a Ferrari Roma or Porsche 911 Turbo S can often be found within 1-4 weeks. Rare specifications or limited-production models may take 1-6 months. We provide realistic timelines upfront and keep you updated throughout the search.

What does exotic car sourcing cost?

Our fee structure is tailored to each engagement and depends on the complexity of the search, the vehicle's value, and the scope of services required. Every engagement begins with a complimentary consultation where we discuss your needs and provide a transparent fee proposal.

Can you source cars from outside Texas?

Absolutely. While many of our acquisitions come from within Texas, we source vehicles nationally and internationally. We handle all logistics including inspection, negotiation, purchase, transport, and delivery to your location in Texas.

Do you handle pre-purchase inspections?

Yes. Every vehicle we source undergoes a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque-specialist mechanic. We also verify vehicle history, title status, service records, and accident history before presenting any car to our clients.
