Why You Need an Advisor for New Luxury Vehicles

The days of walking into a luxury dealership and driving home with your dream car are over. Today's most desirable vehicles — from the Porsche 911 GT3 to the Ferrari Purosangue and Bentley Continental GT Speed — require navigating complex allocation systems, lengthy waitlists, and dealer markups that can add tens of thousands to the MSRP. Provenance cuts through this complexity, leveraging deep dealer relationships across Texas and beyond to secure allocations and factory orders for our clients.

Marques We Specialize In

Our new vehicle acquisition service covers the full spectrum of luxury and exotic marques. We have established relationships with authorized dealers for Porsche, Ferrari, Lamborghini, McLaren, Bentley, Rolls-Royce, Mercedes-AMG, BMW M, Aston Martin, Land Rover, and Maserati. Whether you need a Porsche 911 S/T allocation, a Ferrari 296 GTS build slot, or a Bentley Bentayga EWB custom order, Provenance has the relationships and expertise to make it happen.

Our Process

Every new vehicle acquisition begins with a detailed consultation where we discuss your desired marque, specification, timeline, and budget. We then activate our dealer network to identify the best path to your vehicle — whether that's a factory order, allocation transfer, dealer locate, or strategic waitlist management. We handle all negotiations, ensuring you pay a fair price without the stress of dealer markups or high-pressure sales tactics.

Factory Orders & Custom Specifications

For clients who want to build their dream car from scratch, we guide you through the entire factory order process. From selecting the perfect paint color and interior trim to configuring performance packages and optional equipment, we ensure every detail reflects your vision. We've helped Texas clients configure everything from Paint-to-Sample Porsche 911s to bespoke Rolls-Royce Spectres.

Serving All of Texas

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

New Vehicle Acquisition — FAQ

How do Porsche allocations work in Texas?

Porsche allocations are distributed to authorized dealers based on their sales history and relationship with Porsche Cars North America. High-demand models like the GT3, GT3 RS, and 911 S/T have strict allocation criteria. Provenance navigates this process on your behalf, leveraging our dealer relationships to secure allocations faster and at fair pricing.

Can you help avoid dealer markups on new luxury cars?

Yes. Dealer markups (also called 'market adjustments') are common on high-demand models. Provenance leverages our network of dealer relationships to negotiate MSRP or near-MSRP pricing, saving our clients thousands to tens of thousands of dollars on popular models.

How long does a factory order typically take?

Factory order timelines vary by marque and model. A Porsche factory order typically takes 3-6 months. Ferrari build slots can take 12-24 months. Bentley and Rolls-Royce custom orders range from 6-12 months. Provenance provides realistic timeline estimates and keeps you updated throughout the process.

Do I need to have a purchase history with a dealer to get an allocation?

For some marques like Ferrari, purchase history is important. Provenance can help you build dealership relationships and navigate the path to your first allocation, even if you don't have prior purchase history.
