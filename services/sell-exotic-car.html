COLLECTION DISPOSITION & SALES

Sell Your Exotic Car or Collection

When it's time to part with a vehicle or an entire collection, Provenance manages every detail to maximize your return — from professional presentation to final sale.

Schedule a Free Consultation

Selling Deserves the Same Expertise as Buying

Selling an exotic, luxury, or collectible car is just as complex as buying one — and the stakes are equally high. Price it wrong, present it poorly, or choose the wrong sales channel, and you could leave tens of thousands of dollars on the table. Provenance brings the same depth of market knowledge and attention to detail to the selling process that we bring to acquisitions.

Full-Service Sales Management

Our Collection Disposition service covers every aspect of the selling process. We begin with a comprehensive market valuation based on current comparable sales, market trends, and your vehicle's specific attributes. Then we develop a tailored sales strategy: whether that means auction consignment at a premier house, a targeted private-party sale, or a strategic dealer trade-in, we choose the channel that maximizes your return.

Professional Presentation

First impressions matter enormously in the exotic car market. Provenance coordinates professional detailing, studio-quality photography, and compelling vehicle descriptions that highlight your car's unique qualities. We know exactly what buyers look for — service history documentation, originality, specification details, and provenance — and we present your vehicle in the best possible light.

Auction Consignment

For the right vehicle, auction consignment can achieve exceptional results. Provenance manages the entire process: selecting the appropriate auction house (RM Sotheby's, Gooding & Company, Bonhams, Mecum, or Bring a Trailer), negotiating consignment terms and reserves, coordinating vehicle transport, and monitoring bidding activity. Our market knowledge ensures realistic reserves that attract bidding while protecting your interests.

Private-Party Sales

Many exotic and collectible vehicles sell best through private channels. Provenance taps into our extensive network of qualified buyers — collectors, enthusiasts, and dealers who are actively seeking specific vehicles. We handle all inquiries, qualify buyers, manage viewings, negotiate terms, and coordinate the transaction from offer to title transfer.

Collection Liquidation

For clients looking to sell multiple vehicles or an entire collection, Provenance provides strategic disposition planning. We analyze your collection, determine the optimal timing and sales channel for each vehicle, and execute a phased or comprehensive sales plan designed to maximize total returns. We've helped Texas collectors successfully dispose of collections ranging from three cars to thirty.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Collection Disposition & Vehicle Sales — FAQ

How much does it cost to use Provenance's selling service?

Our selling fees are structured as a percentage of the sale price, incentivizing us to maximize your return. The exact percentage depends on the vehicle's value and the complexity of the engagement. Every engagement begins with a complimentary consultation where we discuss your goals and provide a transparent fee proposal.

How long does it take to sell an exotic car?

Timelines vary based on the vehicle, pricing, and market conditions. Well-priced, desirable vehicles can sell within 2-4 weeks. More niche or higher-value vehicles may take 1-3 months. Auction timelines depend on the auction schedule. Provenance provides realistic timeline estimates based on current market conditions.

Should I sell my exotic car at auction or privately?

The best channel depends on several factors: the vehicle's value, rarity, market demand, your timeline, and your pricing expectations. Auctions offer broad exposure and competitive bidding but come with fees and risk of no-sale. Private sales offer more control and potentially lower costs. Provenance advises on the optimal approach for your specific situation.

Can you help me sell just one car from my collection?

Absolutely. Whether you're selling a single vehicle or an entire collection, Provenance provides the same full-service support. Many of our clients start with a single vehicle and return to us when they're ready to sell additional cars.
