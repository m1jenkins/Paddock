Personal Car Buying Consultant

Your Automotive Advisor, Not a Salesman

Buying a car should be exciting, not stressful. But between aggressive salespeople, confusing financing options, and an overwhelming number of choices, even experienced buyers can feel lost. Provenance's Personal Automotive Advisory service provides objective, expert guidance to help you navigate the car-buying process with confidence. We work for you — never the dealer — ensuring every recommendation is based on what's best for you, not what earns the highest commission.

Who This Service Is For

Our Personal Automotive Advisory is designed for buyers who want expert guidance but aren't necessarily looking for a rare exotic. This service is perfect for: first-time sports car buyers who want to make a smart choice, families looking for the right luxury SUV that balances performance with practicality, professionals seeking a sophisticated daily driver, enthusiasts upgrading to their dream car, and anyone who values having a knowledgeable, independent advisor in their corner.

How It Works

We begin with a detailed conversation about your needs, preferences, lifestyle, and budget. What will you use the car for? How important is performance versus comfort? Do you need all-wheel drive for Texas weather? What about passenger and cargo space? Based on your answers, we develop a shortlist of recommended vehicles across appropriate marques and price points. We then help you evaluate each option — arranging test drives, comparing specifications, and providing our honest assessment of each vehicle's strengths and weaknesses.

Test Drive Coordination & Evaluation

Once we've narrowed your options, Provenance coordinates test drives at dealerships across Texas. We accompany you (in person or virtually) to provide real-time guidance on what to look for and how each car performs relative to your needs. Our goal is to help you make a decision you'll love for years, not just weeks.

Negotiation & Purchase Support

Once you've found your car, Provenance handles the negotiation. We leverage our market knowledge to ensure you pay a fair price, whether you're buying new or pre-owned. We also advise on financing versus cash purchase, lease options, trade-in valuations, and extended warranty considerations — areas where uninformed buyers often overpay.

Beyond the Purchase

Our advisory doesn't end when you drive off the lot. Provenance provides ongoing guidance on maintenance schedules, recommended service providers across Texas, and when to consider selling or upgrading your vehicle. We're your automotive advisor for the long term.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Personal Automotive Advisory — FAQ

What does a personal car buying consultant do?

A personal car buying consultant works exclusively on your behalf to help you find, evaluate, and acquire the right vehicle. Unlike a dealership salesperson who represents the seller, Provenance represents you — providing objective advice, market knowledge, and negotiation expertise to ensure you make the best possible purchase.

How much does a personal car buying consultation cost?

Every engagement begins with a complimentary consultation. Our advisory fees are tailored to the scope of each engagement — from a single consultation to full-service vehicle search and acquisition. We provide transparent fee proposals before any commitment.

Can you help me choose between different types of cars?

Absolutely. That's exactly what this service is designed for. Whether you're choosing between a Porsche Cayenne and a BMW X5 M, a Mercedes-AMG GT and a Porsche 911, or a Range Rover and a Cadillac Escalade, we provide objective, expert guidance based on your specific needs.

Do you help with used car purchases too?

Yes. Our Personal Automotive Advisory covers both new and pre-owned vehicle purchases. For used vehicles, we also conduct thorough pre-purchase inspections, vehicle history verification, and market value analysis to ensure you're making a sound purchase.
