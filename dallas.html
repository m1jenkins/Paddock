Luxury Car Consulting in Dallas

Dallas has long been synonymous with luxury, and its automotive scene is no exception. From the prestigious enclaves of Highland Park and Preston Hollow to the booming corridors of Uptown and the Arts District, Dallas residents demand the very best — including the cars they drive.

As your luxury car consultant in Dallas, Provenance brings decades of automotive expertise to the DFW Metroplex. Whether you're seeking a limited-production Lamborghini Revuelto, a rare-spec Porsche 911 S/T, or a concours-quality classic Ferrari, we source it, vet it, negotiate it, and deliver it — all while you focus on what matters most.

The Dallas–Fort Worth area boasts one of the highest concentrations of exotic car dealerships in the country, from Park Place to Boardwalk Ferrari. But Provenance offers something dealerships can't: complete independence. We work for you, not the seller, ensuring every acquisition is made on your terms.

Dallas's thriving car culture, from Cars and Coffee events in Plano to the annual Concours d'Elegance, makes it one of the most exciting markets in Texas for collectors. Our Dallas clients trust us to navigate this landscape with expertise and discretion.
